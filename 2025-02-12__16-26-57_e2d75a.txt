Clean Technology Manufacturing (CTM) Investment Tax Credit (ITC)
Proposed legislative changes
The federal government has proposed changes to certain provisions of the Income Tax Act related to the Clean Economy ITCs that may come into effect retroactively.
Content on the website reflects the current legislation, and not the proposed changes.
Learn more: Government consults Canadians on Budget 2024 measures
The CTM ITC is a refundable tax credit to encourage the investment of capital for clean technology manufacturing and processing and critical mineral extraction and processing in Canada from January 1, 2024, to December 31, 2034.
The CTM ITC is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). 
CRA provides:
Ability to claim the credit on a corporate income tax return
