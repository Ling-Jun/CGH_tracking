Clean Technology Manufacturing (CTM) Investment Tax Credit (ITC)
The CTM ITC is a refundable tax credit to encourage the investment of capital for clean technology manufacturing and processing and critical mineral extraction and processing in Canada from
January 1, 2024
, to
December 31, 2034
.
The CTM ITC is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).
CRA provides:
Ability to claim the credit on a corporate income tax return
